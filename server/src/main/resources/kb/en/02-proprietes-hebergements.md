# Managing your properties and accommodations in Baitly

## How do I create a new property?

Open the Properties menu and click New property. Enter the property's name (for example "2-bedroom apartment, city center"), its type (apartment, house, villa, studio, loft, duplex, townhouse, bungalow, riad, guest room, rural cottage, chalet, boat or other), then its address: full address, city, postal code, country. An address search field with autocomplete makes data entry easier. Then complete the characteristics: number of bedrooms, bathrooms, surface area in square meters, maximum number of guests, nightly price in euros, and a free-form description (the property's strengths, its surroundings). Click Create property to save it. The property then appears in your portfolio and becomes selectable everywhere: reservations, planning, pricing, cleaning and maintenance interventions.

## What can be found on a property's detail page?

A property's detail page (accessible via View details from the property list) is organized into several tabs: Overview (general information, characteristics, status), Interventions, Maintenance, Cleaning and Photos. You can also review performance indicators such as occupancy rate, ADR (average daily rate) and revenue, as well as the day's operational state: occupied, available, check-out, cleaning in progress, maintenance. The property's status can be Active, Inactive, Maintenance, Rented, Sold or Archived. To modify a property, use the Edit button; to delete it, the Delete button (an irreversible action, with confirmation). A "Visible on the Booking Engine" switch makes the property bookable online through your reservation engine.

## How do I add photos to my property?

On the property's detail page, open the Photos tab and click Add photos, or drag and drop your images directly into the designated area. The maximum size is 10 MB per image. Photos are used to present your property to guests, particularly on the online booking engine, and are synchronized with channel managers (distribution platforms such as Airbnb, Booking.com) once those are connected. You can delete a photo at any time (a confirmation is requested). Make sure to upload representative photos: they are the guest's first visual contact with your accommodation.

## How do I define my property's amenities?

Amenities are defined on the property's detail page, in the Amenities section. They are organized by category: Comfort (WiFi, TV, air conditioning, heating), Kitchen (equipped kitchen), Appliances (dishwasher, microwave, oven, washing machine, dryer, iron, hair dryer), Outdoor (parking, pool, hot tub, garden/terrace, barbecue) and Security & Family (safe, baby cot, high chair). Check everything your accommodation offers: these amenities are displayed to guests and serve as search criteria on the booking engine. If you import listings from external platforms, the amenities detected on your listings can be mapped to the Baitly reference from Settings, OTA Amenities tab, where you can also create your own amenities if nothing matches.

## How do I set capacity, times and the timezone?

On the property's detail page, capacity is set via the Maximum number of guests field, complemented by the number of bedrooms and bathrooms. The default check-in and check-out times are also defined on the detail page: they serve as the reference for the planning and for automatically scheduling cleaning after each departure. The property's timezone is chosen in the address section of the detail page: it determines the property's local time, used for the planning and access codes. This matters if you manage properties in several countries: each property lives in its own local time, independently of your account's timezone.

## How do I configure cleaning for a property?

Each property detail page includes a cleaning pricing section: base price per intervention, estimated duration in minutes, cleaning frequency (after each stay, weekly, on demand, etc.) and free-form cleaning instructions for your teams. Optional add-on services refine the estimate: windows, French doors, bay windows, ironing and folding, deep kitchen cleaning, disinfection. Baitly's cleaning engine calculates a real-time price and duration estimate based on the property's characteristics (surface area, bedrooms, bathrooms, floors, outdoor space, linen provided); you can adopt this recommended price as the property's price in one click, or send a cleaning quote as a PDF to the property's owner. This information then feeds the cleaning interventions created after each check-out.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I manage several properties with a single account?**
Yes, Baitly is designed for multi-property management: the property list, planning and pricing handle your entire portfolio, with filters by owner, type and status.

**How do I make a property bookable online?**
Enable the "Visible on the Booking Engine" option on the property's detail page. It then becomes available for booking on your online reservation engine.

**What happens if I set a property to Maintenance or Inactive status?**
The status reflects the property's state within your portfolio. To block specific dates from being booked (renovation work, owner stay), use a period block from the Planning instead.

**Can my account's timezone and the property's timezone differ?**
Yes. Your personal timezone (Settings, General tab) controls your display; the property's timezone governs the planning and access codes for that property.
