# Paid guest services and the Shop

## What are paid services (add-on options for guests)?

Paid services (also called extras, options or upsells) are additional services you sell to your guests on top of their stay: early check-in, late check-out, extra cleaning, airport transfer, breakfast or welcome basket, parking spot, equipment (such as a baby cot), an experience, or any other option of your choice. They are managed in the Distribution menu, on the Booking & welcome page, under the "Paid services" tab. Each service belongs to a category, has a title, a price and a currency, and can be offered on a specific property or on all your properties. Guests discover these services during their stay (welcome guide) or at booking time (booking engine) and pay directly online by card, through a secure payment.

## How do I create or edit a paid service?

In Booking & welcome, Paid services tab, click "New service". Fill in the category (Early check-in, Late check-out, Cleaning, Transfer, Breakfast, Parking, Equipment, Experience or Other), the title (required), the price (required) and the currency, then choose the relevant property or "All properties". You can add a description, upload an image (it is compressed automatically), and refine the display conditions: a minimum stay in nights and a minimum lead time before arrival in hours (useful for a transfer or a welcome basket that needs preparation). It is also possible to group several offers into a bundle with a combined price. The "Service active" toggle makes the service visible; untick it to withdraw it temporarily without deleting it. To get started quickly, pre-filled templates are offered (early check-in, late check-out, extra cleaning, breakfast basket, airport transfer, parking): one click pre-fills the form, and you only need to adjust the price.

## Where are my services offered to guests (distribution)?

Each service is distributed on two channels, which can be enabled independently from its record. The "Welcome guide" channel shows the service in the services shop of the digital guide guests consult during their stay; on each guide, you can additionally tick or untick the displayed services ("Untick a service to hide it on this guide only"), and a general "Paid services" switch turns the block on or off for that guide. The "Booking Engine" channel offers the service as an extra at payment time for a direct reservation on your website. The service list can also be filtered by channel (Guide or Booking) and by category. For the configuration of your booking website itself (pages, engine, cart), see the documentation on the direct booking engine.

## How do I track sales and guest purchases?

The Paid services tab offers full tracking. At the top of the list, three indicators summarize activity: active services, bookings over 30 days and revenue over 30 days; each service's record details its own performance (bookings, revenue generated, average basket). The "Sales" button opens the list of service sales: for each purchase, you see the service involved, the date, the guest's email, the amount paid and your share. Each sale has a status: Pending, Paid, Cancelled or Refunded. As long as no guest has ordered anything, the list shows "No sale yet". This tracking helps you spot the services that work and adjust pricing and distribution.

## How do I get paid and which commissions apply?

The guest pays for their purchase online by card, through a secure payment, directly from the welcome guide or when booking. The proceeds are then split: the platform takes a commission (a rate set by the platform, displayed for information), and, if you are a concierge business or an agency, you can set your own share of the remainder, with the owner receiving the balance. The "Commissions" button in the Paid services tab shows these rates and lets you set your share (one for services, one for activities). Your share is paid out through your usual payouts. On each sale, the "your share" column shows the amount you receive after commission.

## What is the Shop menu (property equipment)?

Not to be confused with paid services: the Distribution menu's "Shop" entry is a hardware catalog meant for the host themselves, to equip their properties with Baitly-compatible connected devices, such as kits, noise sensors, smart locks and environment sensors. You browse products by category and add them to the cart, with no monthly subscription and free shipping in metropolitan France. In short: the Shop is for buying hardware for your properties, while the Paid services tab of Booking & welcome is for selling services to your guests.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I offer a service on a single property?**
Yes: when creating it, choose the relevant property, or "All properties" for global distribution. You can also hide a service on a specific guide without deactivating it elsewhere.

**How do I withdraw a service temporarily without deleting it?**
Open the service and turn off the "Service active" toggle: it disappears from the guides and the booking engine, and can be reactivated later.

**Where can I see what guests have purchased?**
In Booking & welcome, Paid services tab, "Sales" button: each sale shows the service, the date, the guest's email, the amount, your share and the status (Pending, Paid, Cancelled, Refunded).

**Does the guest need to create an account to buy a service?**
No. From the welcome guide, they pay online by card through a secure payment, without creating an account.

**Can I make a service conditional on the length of the stay?**
Yes: each service can require a minimum stay (in nights) and a minimum lead time before arrival (in hours) to be offered and orderable.
