# The Baitly Directory and Contacts hub

## What is the Contacts hub and what does the Directory contain?

In Baitly's navigation, the "Contacts" entry brings together two spaces: Messaging (conversations and received forms, see the dedicated messaging article) and the Directory. The Directory is the central register of all the people and structures involved in your activity. Depending on your role and permissions, it shows up to six tabs: Users (platform accounts), Teams (operational cleaning, maintenance and supervision teams), Portfolios (property groupings), Organizations (client structures, for the platform team), Guests (the database of clients who stayed in your properties) and Prospecting (the sales pipeline, reserved for the Baitly team). Tabs you are not allowed to see are simply hidden; if only one tab is accessible to you, it is shown directly full page.

## How do I find a guest in the Directory?

Open the Directory menu, Guests tab. The list is populated automatically: a guest appears from their first reservation, whatever the original channel. You have a search by name, email or phone, and a filter by channel (Direct, Airbnb, Booking, VRBO, iCal, Other). For each guest, the table shows: name, email, phone, acquisition channel, number of stays, total spent and record creation date. It is the ideal tool for spotting your loyal clients or finding a contact before creating a direct reservation. The guest record (identity, nationality, language, notes) is linked to reservations: when creating a reservation, you look up an existing guest or create their record on the fly, and this record feeds the police record when regulations require it, see the article dedicated to reservations.

## What are the Teams and Portfolios tabs for?

The Teams tab gathers your operational field teams: composition (technicians, housekeepers, supervisors), specialties and availability. This is where you create a team and add members to it, so that you can then assign an intervention to the whole team rather than to a single person. The Portfolios tab lets you group properties by owner or by management portfolio: a portfolio brings together properties, clients and team members, which makes oversight easier when you manage properties for several owners (the typical case of a concierge business). Historical shortcuts exist: the old Teams, Portfolios or Guests pages automatically redirect to the corresponding Directory tab. How teams work in detail is described in the teams and technicians article.

## Who manages the Users and Organizations tabs?

The Users tab lists the platform accounts: name, email, role, with management actions (creation, role change, activation, access reset). It is only visible if you hold the user management permission, held by administrators. The Organizations tab, reserved for the Baitly platform team, catalogs client organizations with their legal information, branding and configuration: a client manager does not need to worry about it. A useful reminder: to invite a collaborator into your organization, the usual entry point remains Settings, Organization tab (email invitation with role choice); the Directory then serves to view and administer the accounts. Roles and permissions are covered in detail in a dedicated article.

## What is the Prospecting tab?

The Prospecting tab is a sales module reserved for the Baitly platform team (it does not appear for client organizations). It embodies a prospecting pipeline: importing prospects from a CSV file, sorting by category and status, tracking contact details (email, website, LinkedIn) and enriching records. If you do not see this tab in your Directory, that is normal: your role does not give access to this function, which is not part of day-to-day rental management. No action is required on your part; your own business contacts (guests, owners, providers) are managed through the Guests, Portfolios and Users tabs described above.

## Frequently asked questions

**Where is the list of my guests?**
In the Directory menu (the Contacts entry in the navigation), Guests tab: search by name, email or phone, filter by channel, with the number of stays and the total spent per client.

**Can I add a guest manually?**
The database fills itself automatically at each guest's first reservation. You create a new guest record when entering a direct reservation if the client does not exist yet.

**Why are some Directory tabs invisible to me?**
Display depends on your permissions: for example, Users requires user management (administrators), and Organizations as well as Prospecting are reserved for the Baitly platform team.

**What is the difference between the Directory and Messaging?**
Both live in the Contacts hub: the Directory is the register (who my guests, teams and users are), Messaging is the feed of conversations and received forms.

**How do I group the properties of a single owner?**
Create a portfolio in the Directory, Portfolios tab, then attach the properties, the owner client and the relevant team members to it.
