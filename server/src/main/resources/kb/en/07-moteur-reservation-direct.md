# Direct booking engine (website, Studio, widget, promos)

## What is the Baitly direct booking engine?

The Booking Engine lets you sell your stays directly, without platform commission. Two use cases: create a full booking website with the Baitly Studio, or embed the booking widget on your existing website (WordPress, Wix, custom site...). In both cases, the guest browses the properties you have published, checks availability in real time (the same calendar as your Airbnb, Booking and other channels), sees the detailed price and pays online. A direct reservation immediately blocks the dates on all your connected channels. Configuration is done from the Booking Engine page in the menu: Templates and Configuration tabs, with the General, Appearance, Settings, Customization, Options and Integration steps.

## How do I create my booking website with the Studio?

The Studio is the booking engine's no-code editor. It offers two editing modes: Guided (essential blocks, colors and typography, ideal for a quick start) and Advanced (a full editor with all blocks, layers, settings and design import). You can also generate your website with AI: describe your business (property types, desired tone, brand name, main color, languages), and the AI writes and structures a complete website — home, property list, about, contact — with a derived theme. Generated pages are created as drafts, to be reviewed before publishing. A design analysis assistant can also draw inspiration from your existing website to derive colors and styles (design tokens), and ready-to-use design presets are available.

## How do I choose a template from the gallery?

The Studio's template gallery ("All templates") offers ready-to-use website models: pick a template to start your booking engine, then customize it freely in the Studio (texts, images, colors, blocks). The instantiated template becomes your website: you can then retouch it manually in Guided or Advanced mode, or request AI-powered retouches. Each organization can create several booking engine configurations (for example one website per brand or per destination), each with its own template, published properties and settings.

## How do I embed the booking widget on my existing website?

If you already have a website, embed the Baitly widget: the configuration page provides an integration code to copy and paste (script or iframe), which works on any technology. The widget follows your theme (colors, font, logo) defined in the configuration. On the security side, each engine has a public key and a list of authorized domains: only the websites you have declared can display the widget. You choose, property by property, what is visible on the booking engine, as well as the components displayed (search bar, gallery, availability calendar, price breakdown, reviews, map, cancellation policy...). Settings define the booking window (minimum and maximum lead time before arrival), the default currency and language.

## What does the booking flow look like for the guest?

The guest searches by dates and number of guests, browses an availability calendar, then gets a detailed price: nightly rate, cleaning fees, tourist tax and total. They can add several stays to a cart, enter a promo code, sign in, choose paid options and then pay online by card securely. The reservation is first created as pending: if payment is not completed within 30 minutes, it expires and the dates are released. After payment, the guest receives a confirmation email, an invoice is generated automatically and the dates are synchronized to your other channels. The website can be offered in several languages (French, English, Arabic): the Studio translates pages with AI, with the variants created as drafts to be reviewed before publishing.

## How do I create a promo code (voucher)?

The Promos & Vouchers page in the menu manages promo codes and discount campaigns applied to the booking engine's nightly prices. Two types: manual code (the guest enters the code) or automatic campaign (discount applied automatically). The discount is a percentage or a fixed amount. You define the validity period, the property scope (all or a selection), the channel (all, booking engine, direct link), a minimum or maximum stay, a minimum amount and total and per-guest usage caps. A voucher can be paused and reactivated; a voucher that has already been used can no longer be deleted (pause it instead). An analytics panel shows uses, gross revenue, the discount granted and the net. The guest sees the discount before paying; an invalid code displays a clear reason (expired, cap reached, property out of scope...).

## How do I sell additional options and services (upsells)?

You can offer paid services to your guests: early check-in, late check-out, extra cleaning, transfer, breakfast, parking, equipment, experience... Each option has a title, a price, an image and can be attached to a specific property or to all properties. Active options are offered to the guest during the direct booking and paid together with the stay; an order tracking view shows their status (pending, paid, cancelled, refunded) and your share. Service options by category also exist, priced per reservation, per person or per night.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to know how to code to build my booking website?**
No. The Studio is a no-code editor: template gallery, Guided mode, AI website generation. Advanced mode remains available to go further.

**What happens if the guest does not pay?**
The pending reservation expires after 30 minutes and the dates are automatically released.

**Can I restrict a promo code to a single property?**
Yes. Each voucher has a scope: all the organization's properties or a specific selection.

**Can my website be in English and Arabic?**
Yes. The Studio handles several languages per page, with AI translation created as drafts to be reviewed before publishing.

**Can a direct reservation create a double booking with Airbnb?**
No: availability is checked in real time against the central calendar and, once paid, the reservation blocks the dates on the connected channels.
