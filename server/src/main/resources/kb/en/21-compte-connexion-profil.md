# Account, sign-in and personal profile

## How do I sign in to Baitly?

You sign in on the Baitly login page. Enter your email (or your username) and your password, then click "Sign in". An eye-shaped icon lets you show or hide the password while typing. After several failed attempts, an additional security check may appear and the account can be temporarily locked: wait a few minutes before trying again. If you do not have an account yet, the "Create one" link at the bottom of the page opens the registration flow, and the "Contact support" link lets you ask for help. Your credentials are the same on the web application and on the Baitly mobile app.

## What should I do if I forgot my password?

On the login page, a "Forgot your password?" link is available under the password field. If you cannot recover access to your account this way, contact Baitly support (the "Contact support" link at the bottom of the login page) or reach out to your organization's administrator: they can check your account and help you restore access. To avoid lockouts, first make sure you are using the right email (the one you were invited or registered with) and that caps lock is not on. Note that the password is set when the account is created (registration or invitation acceptance): there is no password change field in the profile settings.

## How do I create a Baitly account (registration)?

From the login page, click "Create one" to open the registration form. After submitting the form, a confirmation email is sent to you: open it and follow the link to finalize the creation of your account. This is the step where you create your password (8 characters minimum), with a confirmation, using the "Create my password" button. Once the password is created, you can sign in normally. If you are joining an existing organization (a concierge business or an agency), you do not need to register on your own: instead, wait for the invitation email sent by the organization's manager (see the section on invitations).

## Where can I find and edit my profile (My account)?

Your personal information lives in Settings, General tab, in the "My account" card. To get there quickly, click your user card (avatar, name and role) at the bottom of the left sidebar: it opens Settings directly. The "My account" card shows your first name, last name, username and email: these identity fields are read-only (to have them corrected, contact an administrator of your organization, who manages user records, or support). You can, however, edit your company name, timezone, currency and language there. Your profile picture (avatar) appears on this card and in the sidebar; updating it goes through the organization's user management.

## How do I change the language, currency and light or dark theme?

The quickest way is the preferences menu at the bottom of the left sidebar (the "Appearance, language & currency" button). It gathers three sections: Appearance (choice of an accent color and of Light, Dark or Automatic mode), Language (French, English or Arabic, and the change is immediate) and Currency (the display currency for amounts). The same settings also exist in Settings, General tab: the "My account" card for language and currency, and the "Display" card for light, dark or automatic mode (Automatic follows your device's setting). These preferences are personal: they are saved on your account, and you get the same settings back when you sign in from another device.

## How do I sign out of Baitly?

The logout button sits at the bottom of the left sidebar, next to your user card: it is the icon whose tooltip reads "Logout". One click securely closes your session and takes you back to the login page. Remember to sign out when using a shared computer. Your session also expires automatically after a long period of inactivity: simply sign back in with your usual credentials. Signing out does not delete any data and does not change your preferences (language, theme, currency), which are restored the next time you sign in.

## How do I accept an invitation to join an organization?

When a manager invites you to their organization, you receive an email containing an invitation link. Opening it shows a Baitly page summarizing the invitation: the organization's name, the role offered to you (for example Host, Technician, Housekeeper), the invited email and the invitation's expiration date. Two cases are possible. If you do not have an account yet, click "Create an account and accept": fill in your first name, last name, phone (optional, useful for notifications) and a password (8 characters minimum), the email is the invited one and cannot be changed, then confirm with "Create my account and accept". If you already have an account, choose "I already have an account", sign in, and the invitation is accepted automatically if the email matches; a short "Complete your profile" screen may ask for your phone number. In both cases, a "Welcome!" message confirms that you have joined the organization and you are redirected to the planning.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I change my sign-in email myself?**
No, the email and username are read-only in "My account". Ask an administrator of your organization or support to make the change.

**Does my chosen language apply to my whole organization?**
No, it is a personal setting: each user picks their own language (French, English or Arabic), display currency and theme.

**My invitation has expired, what should I do?**
An invitation has an expiration date shown on the invitation page. If it has passed or the invitation is invalid, ask the organization's manager to send you a new one.

**I am signed in with a different account than the invited one, how do I accept the invitation?**
The invitation page detects this and tells you: use the button provided to sign out, then accept the invitation with the matching email.
