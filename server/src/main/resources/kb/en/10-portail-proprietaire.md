# Owner portal and shared tracking

## What is the Baitly owner portal?

The owner portal is the space that lets a property management company or manager report back to its owners: revenue, reservations, occupancy and statements, owner by owner. It has two sides: on the manager's side, the "Owner Portal" page of the PMS (dashboard and statements per owner); on the owner's side, a public, view-only tracking page ("Owner space"), accessible through a simple shared link, with no account creation or password. It is the typical transparency tool for a property management business: the owner sees what their properties generate and what has been done for them, without accessing the rest of the PMS.

## What does an owner see on their shared tracking space?

The public "Owner space" page is view-only. It presents the key financial indicators of the owner's portfolio: gross revenue, commissions, owner net and occupancy rate. It also includes a "What our agents did for your properties" section covering the last 30 days: the number of actions and suggestions carried out automatically or by the team, detailed property by property (with the note "Nothing to report on this property" when nothing happened). The page ends with "Report prepared by" followed by your organization's name. The owner cannot modify anything: it is a living report, not access to the PMS.

## How do I share the tracking link with an owner?

From the PMS Owner Portal page, select the relevant owner and use the "Share owner tracking" button: the public link is copied to the clipboard ("Link copied!" message), to be sent to the owner by email or messaging. This link is unique to each owner and only gives access to their own viewing space. A link can become invalid or expire: the owner then sees "Invalid or expired link — this tracking link is no longer active, contact your property manager to get a new one"; simply re-share a link from the same page. Treat this link as confidential: anyone who has it can view the figures of the owner concerned.

## How do I brand the owner page with my identity?

The public page can be dressed in your property management company's colors via "Customize the owner page" (owner page — your brand): enter the URL of your logo (over HTTPS; leave the field empty if you prefer not to display a logo) and an accent color in hexadecimal format. The owner then sees a report in your company's image rather than a generic page. This customization applies to the shared tracking space of all your owners.

## What does the owner dashboard contain on the manager's side?

In the PMS, the Owner Portal page offers two tabs once an owner is selected. The Dashboard tab displays their indicators: number of properties, active reservations, net revenue, average occupancy and average rating, a revenue-by-month chart, and a table per property (revenue, occupancy, number of reservations). The Statement tab generates an owner statement over a chosen period (owner name, from and to dates): it is the summary document of revenue and payouts to send to the owner. The payouts themselves (pending, approved, paid cycle) are tracked on the Billing page, Payouts tab.

## How do I organize my owners into portfolios?

The Portfolios page structures your management business: create client portfolios attached to a manager, associate clients (owners) and their properties with them, then associate operational teams and users (technicians, housekeepers, supervisors). Three tabs: My Portfolios, Team Management and Statistics (number of portfolios, clients, properties). A step-by-step wizard guides the association: choose the manager, choose the clients, the properties, then optionally the teams and users. You can later reassign or detach a client, a property or a team. Each owner keeps their individual tracking space; the portfolio serves the internal organization of the management company.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does my owner need to create an account to see their revenue?**
No. You share a personal tracking link with them: they view their owner space directly, read-only, with no account or password.

**What should I do if the tracking link no longer works?**
The link has expired or been invalidated. Go back to the Owner Portal page, select the owner and re-share a link.

**Can the owner see other owners' data?**
No. Each link is unique to one owner and only shows their properties, their revenue and the activity that concerns them.

**What is the difference between gross revenue and owner net?**
Gross revenue is the total generated by the stays; owner net is what goes to the owner after deducting the commissions set out in their management contract.

**How do I send a formal statement to my owner?**
From the Owner Portal page, Statement tab: choose the owner and the period, then generate the statement summarizing revenue and payouts.
