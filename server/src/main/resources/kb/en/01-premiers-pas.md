# Getting started with Baitly

## What is Baitly and who is the platform for?

Baitly is a short-term rental property management system (PMS) built for owners, property management companies and concierge businesses. It centralizes the management of your properties, reservations, multi-property calendar, rates, guests, cleaning and maintenance interventions, billing and guest communication. Every user works within an organization: your organization brings together your properties, your team members and your settings. The available roles cover the full range of jobs: Host (owner), Supervisor, Technician, Housekeeper, Laundry, Exterior Tech, plus platform administration roles (Super Admin, Super Manager). Depending on your role, the visible menus and permitted actions differ: a technician or a housekeeper only sees the interventions assigned to them, while a host manages all of their properties and reservations.

## How do I set up my organization when getting started?

On your first login, Baitly guides you through a list of initial setup steps (onboarding): configure the organization (name, address, logo, contact details), invite members, adjust general settings (currency, timezone, language, display preferences), fill in the fiscal profile (company registration number, VAT, legal name, legal notices), configure notifications and messaging, create your first property, define your pricing and connect your booking channels (Airbnb, Booking or others). You can skip certain steps and come back to them later from Settings. Your organization's information (legal details, branding, team and permissions) is managed in Settings, under the Organization tab.

## How do I invite my team and manage members?

Inviting collaborators is done from Settings, under the Organization tab, using the Invite a member button: enter the person's email address, choose their role, then send the invitation. The person receives an email inviting them to join your organization and creates their access by accepting it. From the member list, you can later change a member's role or remove them from the organization. Each member has a role within the organization that determines their rights: for example, a housekeeper has access to cleaning and inventory, a technician to maintenance interventions, a supervisor to team supervision. Field team management (grouping technicians and housekeepers into teams with coverage areas) is handled in the Directory menu, under the Teams tab.

## How do I set the language, currency and timezone?

Go to Settings, General tab (identity, organization, regional preferences and display sections). This is where you define the timezone, currency and interface language. Baitly is available in French, English and Arabic. These preferences are saved on your account and follow you across all your devices. Note that each property also has its own timezone, defined on its detail page: that property timezone is the reference for the planning, check-in and check-out times and access codes. Light or dark display mode and other visual preferences are also configured in this same General tab.

## What are the main menus in Baitly?

Navigation is organized into major groups. In the main section: Dashboard (key indicators), Assistant (AI-powered help), Properties (your property portfolio with the Dynamic Pricing, Promo Codes and Connected Objects tabs), Reservations, Planning (multi-property calendar), Interventions and Service Requests (cleaning, maintenance). The Directory menu gathers your contacts: Teams, Portfolios, Guests, Users and Organizations. On the finance side: Payments, Billing, Accounting and Invoices. On the distribution side: Channels (connection to platforms such as Airbnb, Booking.com) and Booking Engine (online reservation engine for your own website). Added to these are Messaging (guest communication), Documents & Communications, Reports, Management Contracts and Settings. The menus you see depend on your role.

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I change the interface language?**
In Settings, General tab: choose French, English or Arabic in the Language field. The change applies immediately.

**Can I have several people with different roles in my organization?**
Yes. Invite each collaborator from Settings, Organization tab, and assign them the appropriate role (supervisor, technician, housekeeper, etc.). You can change a role at any time.

**Where can I find the onboarding steps again if I skipped them?**
All settings remain accessible at any time in Settings (General, Organization, Fiscal, Notifications, Messaging, Payment, Integrations tabs).

**Does a technician see all reservations?**
No. Operational roles (technician, housekeeper, laundry) only see the interventions assigned to them, either individually or through their team.
